Policy Version 1 (Old)

1.1. Employees must use AI tools approved by the IT department.

1.2. Personal AI assistants are strictly prohibited on company devices.

1.3. All AI-generated content for external use must be reviewed by a manager.

1.4. Confidential company information should not be entered into public AI models.

1.5. The use of generative AI for code development requires departmental head approval.

Policy Version 2 (New)

1.1. Employees must use AI tools from the pre-approved vendor list maintained by the IT & Security department.

1.3. All AI-generated content for external communications must be reviewed and approved by the department head.

1.4. Confidential or proprietary company information must never be entered into public, non-vetted AI models.

1.5. All AI system usage is subject to logging and auditing for compliance purposes.

1.6. A risk assessment must be completed before integrating any new AI tool into business workflows.
